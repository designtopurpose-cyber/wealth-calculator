What does SARS take?
Know before you retire.

South Africa's tax rules for investors are complex. The Tax Analysis tool applies CGT inclusion rates, your marginal tax rate, and dividends withholding tax to your projected portfolio — so you plan with the after-tax number.

  • CGT at 40% inclusion, up to 18% effective rate
  • 20% dividends withholding tax on income
  • Adjustable growth vs income split
  • Hover tooltips explain every SARS rate
