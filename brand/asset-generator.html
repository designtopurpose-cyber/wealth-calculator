On-brand assets at exact platform dimensions, rendered from brand-guide.md §6 colours and fonts. Wait ~1 second for the Google Fonts to load (canvases below should render correctly), then click each download button. Canvases below are preview-sized; the downloaded PNGs are full resolution.

Reusable for: Instagram, Threads, TikTok avatars

All four use a square avatar — use the same Profile picture PNG above (Instagram displays at 320×320, Threads/TikTok at similar). No separate generation needed.

To add a new asset (e.g. LinkedIn personal banner at 1584×396, IG Reels cover, ad creative): copy a <canvas> block in the HTML, update the id, width, and height, then add a draw… call in the script with the right headline/tagline fractions. Brand colours live as constants at the top of the script.
